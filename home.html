About the game

The Rugby Union World Cup Super 6 (the game) will be played for each round of pool games (20th September - 13th October) 6 games will be selected at random from each round Each group has 5 teams therefore a round is 2.5 fixtures * 4 groups = 10 fixtures Because of scheduling one round has 11 and one round has 9 fixtures, this has no effect on the game
